Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recover a lost trip code?

Unfortunately, trip codes cannot be recovered if lost. We recommend sharing the code with multiple group members and taking a screenshot for backup.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and don't store any sensitive financial information like bank details or payment methods.

Can I use Tripplit for business expenses?

Absolutely! Tripplit works great for team lunches, client entertainment, project expenses, and any group business spending.

How many people can join one trip?

There's no strict limit, but for optimal performance, we recommend keeping groups under 20 people per trip.

Do you have a mobile app?

Not yet, but our web app works perfectly on mobile browsers! It's fully responsive and optimized for touch devices.

Can I export my expense data?

Yes! You can export your trip expenses as a CSV file for your records or accounting purposes.
