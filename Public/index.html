How Tripplit Works

Simple steps to fair expense sharing - no complex setup required

1

Create Your Trip

Give your expense group a name - could be "Bali Adventure", "Office Lunch", or "Roommate Bills". Get a unique 6-character trip code instantly.

Example: Trip: "Weekend Getaway" → Code: ABC123
2

Add Participants

Share your trip code with friends, family, or colleagues. They join instantly - no accounts needed. Everyone can add themselves with just a name.

Participants: Sarah, Mike, Alex, Jennifer
3

Log Expenses

Add expenses with category, amount, and who paid. Choose equal split or customize who owes what. Categories help organize different types of spending.

🏨 Hotel: $320 (split equally)
🍽️ Dinner: $85 (custom split)
🚗 Gas: $45 (exclude 1 person)
4

Automatic Balance Summary

Tripplit instantly calculates who owes what to whom. Smart algorithms minimize the number of transactions needed to settle all debts fairly.

Sarah +$45.50 Gets paid
Mike -$23.75 Owes Sarah
Alex -$21.75 Owes Sarah
5

Easy Settlement

Get clear instructions on exactly who should pay whom. Mark payments as complete to keep everyone updated. No more confusion or awkward conversations about money.

Mike pays Sarah $23.75 ✓ Mark as Paid
Alex pays Sarah $21.75 ⏳ Pending
